Subscription and Purchase Terms

Last Updated: February 27, 2026

By upgrading to a Premium Membership in App2VR, you agree to the following billing terms and conditions. Please read them carefully.

1. Membership Plans

App2VR offers three types of Premium access to suit your needs:

  • Monthly Subscription: Full access to all premium features, billed recurringly every month.
  • Yearly Subscription: Full access to all premium features, billed recurringly every year.
  • Lifetime Membership: A one-time purchase that grants permanent access to all current and future premium features. No recurring fees apply.

2. Billing and Payments

  • Payment Processing: All transactions are processed securely via the Google Play Store. Payment will be charged to your Google Account immediately upon confirmation of purchase.
  • Automatic Renewal: For Monthly and Yearly plans, your subscription will automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.
  • Renewal Charges: Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period at the then-current subscription rate.

3. Managing and Canceling Subscriptions

You have full control over your subscriptions. To manage or cancel your service, please follow these steps provided by Google Play:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app on your Android device.
  2. Tap your Profile Icon at the top right.
  3. Tap Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions.
  4. Select App2VR.
  5. Tap Cancel subscription.

Note: Uninstalling the App2VR app will not automatically stop your subscription. You must manually cancel it through the steps above.

4. Lifetime Membership Policy

The Lifetime Membership is a Managed Product (one-time in-app purchase). It does not expire and does not require cancellation. If you switch devices, you can use the "Restore Purchase" feature within the app to reactivate your access.

5. Refund Policy

Refunds are governed by the Google Play Store Policy. Generally, charges are non-refundable for partial subscription periods. If you believe you are eligible for a refund, you may apply through your Google Play Order History or contact our support team.
